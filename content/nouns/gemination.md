Word ending with -gaq, -raq, -seq, -leq, -iak, -iaq like meeraq, oqaaseq, kalaaleq, double the stem consonant when taking an ending: oqaatimik, meerramik (but not when being derived: oqaatiinnaq)

* meeraq -nik -> meeqqanik
* meeraq -nut -> meeqqanut
* atuagaq -it -> atuakkat
* taseq -it -> tatsit
* najugaq -mi -> najukkami
