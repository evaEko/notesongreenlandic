To express that a noun belongs to the subject of the clause,
the noun takes *+gi*: nuliaq + gi -> `nuliari`

~~~~~~
ikingutigivara: he/she is my friend
nuliaq + gi -laar +tigit -> nuliarilaartigit
~~~~~~
