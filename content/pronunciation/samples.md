# Vowels

1. **Two vowels**  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;long
2. **o** and **u**, and **i** and **e**
   * &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;u becomes o if followed by q
   * &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;i becomes e if followed by q
   Hence "uq" and "iq" don't ever occur.
